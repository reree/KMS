Introduction

KMS is a company which manufactures components of automotive and electronics industry. We specializes in metal forming, assembling and painting. Our product includes components of motorcycles, diesel engines, air-conditioners, and water pumps.

KMS was established in 9th January 2003 as a sister company of Baja Kurnia, a steel casting company. We are committed to meet customer demand by delivering quality product. We believe that customer satisfaction results in customersâ€™ trust and with that trust we can be successful.
